Height Boost 90-Day Plan

Daily Routine

Daily Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: 2-3 eggs, 1 banana, almonds/walnuts, 1 glass milk (cow/soy/almond)
  • Lunch: Dal/Chole/Rajma, Paneer, brown rice/roti, salad, yogurt
  • Snack: Peanut butter toast, fruit bowl (mango, papaya, guava), roasted chana/makhana
  • Dinner: Light sabzi, 2 roti / 1 bowl rice, paneer or tofu, turmeric milk before bed

Posture & Sleep Tips

  • Sleep 8.5-10 hours, on your back
  • Use flat pillow or none
  • No screens 1 hour before bed
  • Practice wall posture (5 mins/day)
  • Avoid slouching during sitting/standing
